Project

Website that lists and describes all the clubs offered in AIS

Objectives

  • Easy for students to use the website.
  • Organized so the students can find what they need
  • Lists and describes all the clubs in AIS.
  • Has contact information for each club for students interested.
  • Includes pictures/videos of what is happening in each club

Success Criteria

  • Website shows the clubs available for AIS students
  • Has videos and photos for each club

Questions

  • Who will be the audience for your website?
    The audience is students in AIS who are interested in joining a club.
  • What will be the specific purpose of your website?
    The purpose is for AIS students to find a club they are interested in.
  • Who is the client?
    The client is Ms. Kruse.
  • Identify the content and major categories and sub-categories for your website.
    The major categories are the club categories. The sub categories are the clubs.
