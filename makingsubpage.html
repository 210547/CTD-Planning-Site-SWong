Flowchart Review

We are still using the flowchart chosen before. However now we do not need a website that has a list of all the clubs
because we are adding a navigation bar that will list the clubs when hovered on.

Website Look and Feel

The message that our website is giving out is to help you find clubs that you are interested in

Our theme for our website is to have the background in light gray,
the text white, and any other boxes greyish blue

We wanted our website to have this specific look and feel because we used cool colors to make the website
look clean and still have color. If we used sharp colors such as bright red it would distract the
audience.

Example of Website Look and Feel

Essential parts of the website to be on every page: The title of the website on the top, the AIS logo under the title, and the navigation bar.
